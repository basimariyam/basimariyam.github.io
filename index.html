About

Let me introduce myself.

Hi I'm Basith. I am an Illustrator cum Graphic Designer with seven years of experience. Possesses a high degree of creativity and familiarity with a variety of print and digital mediums. Experienced Iwith Adobe Creative Suite, particularly with Photoshop and Illustrator, Apple Procreate and Coreldraw and have vast knowledge of illustration and typography. Throughout this time I have illustrated work for publishing companies, though for the past one year I also illustrated children's books. I love painting and bringing characters to life. My work is sensitive, colorful and fun. I have worked for Publishers like Kerala State Institute of Children's Literature and National Book stall (SPCS)
